ABOUT US

Rackonnect is a hyperlocal sports - tech platform aiming to bring a powerful impact on India's racket sports fraternity . We wish to make racket sports accessible to everyone regardless of their age , gender , and economic background . Combining sports and technology , we are focusing on grassroots - level playerand helping them achieve their dreams.In order to improve the way racket sports is played and its Infrastructure in India , Rackonnect has also entered the racket sports Infrastructure space with it'smultiple facilities .

OUR STORY

Sports is a great way to connect people . It brings people together and creates long - lasting memories . The sports industry is booming now , but yet we found a void in the market , i.e using a platform where everyone and anyone can be a part of this community and feel good about themselves.Our mission is to build a vast sports community , inspire & motivate individuals to pursue racket sports at a higher level . We wish to create awareness about racket sports and make racket sports amenities accessible to all . We aim to provide a competitive platform to racket sports enthusiasts through tournaments .
